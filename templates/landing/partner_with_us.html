Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our partnership program

How does the referral commission work?

Our referral partners earn a 20% commission on the first year's subscription for each client they refer who signs up for AI CashOrbit. Commissions are paid monthly and tracked through your partner dashboard.

What resources do you provide to partners?

We provide our partners with marketing materials, product training, dedicated support, and access to a partner portal where you can track referrals and commissions. Integration partners also receive technical documentation and API access.

How long does the application process take?

The application review process typically takes 3-5 business days. Once approved, you'll receive access to our partner portal and all the resources you need to get started right away.

Are there any costs to join the partner program?

No, there are no costs to join our partner program. We believe in creating mutually beneficial relationships that help both our partners and their clients succeed.
